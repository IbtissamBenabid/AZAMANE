# Background Assets for AZAMANE

This directory will contain:

## Scene Backgrounds (320x180 pixels)
- desert_outpost_welcome.png - Welcome screen background
  * Sand dunes, tents, oasis in distance
  * Subtle djinn glow effects
  * Warm desert atmosphere

- desert_outpost_main.png - Main game world background  
  * Trade stalls, oasis, merchant tents
  * Clear hotspot areas for interaction
  * Authentic 7th-century Berber setting

- character_creation_bg.png - Persona customization background
  * Simple, non-distracting backdrop
  * Focus on character preview area

- narrative_scroll_bg.png - Story introduction background
  * Parchment or desert scene
  * Readable text overlay area

## Visual Elements
- Authentic Berber architectural details
- Desert landscape with proper perspective
- Cultural accuracy in tent and stall designs
- Atmospheric lighting suggesting time of day

## Color Palette
- Primary: Desert Brown (#8B5523) for sand and earth
- Accent: Deep Blue (#2A3F7B) for sky and shadows  
- Highlight: Moroccan Gold (#D4A017) for precious items
- Nature: Oasis Green (#355E3B) for vegetation
